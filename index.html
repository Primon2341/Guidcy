About Guidcy

We're building India's most trusted bridge between world-class expertise and the people who need it most.

Guidcy was founded in Hyderabad with a simple belief: expert knowledge should be accessible to everyone — not just those with elite networks or corporate budgets. Whether you're a first-generation entrepreneur, a student navigating career choices, or a professional seeking legal clarity, the right guide can transform your path.

We've built a marketplace where India's top consultants — former McKinsey advisors, IIT CTOs, certified therapists, chartered accountants, and more — offer their expertise in structured, affordable sessions with guaranteed quality.

Our mission

To democratise access to expert guidance by creating a transparent, technology-first consultation marketplace that works for both consultants and clients — with trust, fairness, and impact at its core.

Our values

  • Verified trust — Every consultant is background-checked and credential-verified before going live.
  • Transparent pricing — No hidden fees, no surprises. What you see is what you pay.
  • Outcome-driven — We measure success by real impact on our users' lives and businesses.
  • Accessible expertise — Sessions from ₹500 make quality guidance reachable for everyone.

Terms & Conditions

Last updated: April 2025

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using Guidcy, you agree to be bound by these Terms & Conditions. If you do not agree, please do not use our platform.

2. Platform Description

Guidcy is a consultation marketplace that connects clients with verified independent consultants. Guidcy facilitates the connection but is not a party to the consultation agreement.

3. User Accounts

You must provide accurate, complete information when creating your account. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials.

4. Bookings & Cancellations

Sessions must be cancelled at least 24 hours in advance for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a 50% cancellation fee.

5. Platform Fees

Guidcy charges a 15% platform commission on all completed sessions. This is deducted from the consultant's earnings, not charged to the client separately.

6. Prohibited Conduct

Users may not exchange personal contact information to bypass the platform, post false reviews, or use the platform for any illegal purpose.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (name, email, phone), information from your use of the platform (booking history, session data, reviews), and technical data (device type, IP address, browser).

How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate bookings and sessions between clients and consultants
  • To send transactional notifications (booking confirmations, reminders)
  • To improve our platform and personalise your experience
  • To prevent fraud and ensure platform safety

Data Security

We use 256-bit SSL encryption for all data in transit and AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest. Payment information is handled exclusively by Razorpay and is never stored on Guidcy servers.

Your Rights

Under India's DPDP Act 2023, you have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data. Contact privacy@guidcy.com to exercise these rights.

Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Full Refunds

You are eligible for a full refund if: you cancel at least 24 hours before the scheduled session; the consultant cancels or does not show up; or a technical issue prevents the session from taking place.

Partial Refunds

A 50% refund may be issued for cancellations made between 12 and 24 hours before the session.

No Refunds

No refund will be issued for cancellations made less than 12 hours before the session, sessions that have already commenced, or sessions completed as scheduled.

Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

Disclaimer

Important information about the nature of consultations on Guidcy.

Consultations provided through Guidcy are for informational and advisory purposes only. They do not constitute professional legal, medical, financial, or psychological advice in a formal client-advisor relationship unless explicitly agreed upon separately.

Guidcy does not guarantee any specific outcomes from consultations. The expertise and credentials of consultants are self-reported and verified to the best of our ability, but Guidcy cannot guarantee their accuracy in all cases.

For medical emergencies, please contact emergency services immediately. For urgent legal matters, please consult a licensed attorney directly.
